# Roadmap 200 Years: Library of Babylon

## Visi Jangka Panjang
Library of Babylon adalah arsip digital 200 tahun yang melestarikan karya kreator digital ephemeral—terutama VTuber, musisi indie, dan streamer—yang karyanya akan HILANG saat mereka pensiun atau platform mati. Kami menggunakan AI autonomous (Seeker & Guardian) untuk crawling, archiving, dan self-maintenance selama 2 abad. Misi: memastikan keindahan digital tidak mati bersama penciptanya.

## Timeline Fase Pengembangan

### Phase 1: Foundation (2024-2028)
- ✅ **2024:** Comet archived (DONE), 5 more Suisei songs, folder structure created
- **2025:** Complete Suisei archive (100+ songs), metadata documented, website V1 (static)
- **2026:** Add 5 more creators (Calli, Gura, Indie musicians), prove scalability
- **2027:** Community launch (Reddit, Discord—r/VTuberArchive), 10 volunteer archivers
- **2028:** 50 creators archived, website V2 (search, API), Seeker AI prototype (basic crawling)

### Phase 2: Growth (2028-2033)
- **2029:** Seeker AI operational (autonomous archival), IPFS migration begins
- **2030:** 100 creators archived, community 1,000+ members, first academic paper published
- **2031:** Guardian AI prototype (basic self-repair), grants/funding secured ($500K)
- **2032:** 250 creators archived, platform partnerships (Hololive, indie labels)
- **2033:** 500 creators archived, Guardian AI operational, decentralization 50% complete

### Phase 3: Maturity (2033-2050)
- **2035:** 1,000 creators, $5M endowment, Guardian AI fully autonomous
- **2040:** 2,000 creators, $20M endowment, DNA storage pilot (first 100 creators)
- **2045:** 5,000 creators, $50M endowment, succession training (next generation archivers)
- **2050:** 10,000 creators, full autonomy achieved

### Phase 4+: Perpetuity (2050-2224)
- Library runs autonomously, adapts to new tech, survives founders
- 2100: 50,000 creators, Guardian AI adapted to quantum computing
- 2150: 200,000 creators, DNA storage standard, AI self-upgraded 20x
- 2224: **200-YEAR ANNIVERSARY**—Library preserved 1M+ creators, Guardian AI still running
